# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, precise, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Keep public discussions free of secrets and sensitive operational details.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate exposure of private
information, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through GitHub.
The maintainer may edit, reject, or remove contributions and temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening,
or harmful.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1, available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html.
